Pro Tips

Travel Smarter, Not Harder

Expert advice to make every trip safer, cheaper, and more memorable.

01

Book Flights 6–8 Weeks Early

International fares from India drop significantly when booked 6–8 weeks in advance. Use incognito mode to prevent price hikes.

02

Get Travel Insurance

A ₹500–₹2,000 travel insurance policy can save lakhs in medical emergencies abroad. Never skip this step.

03

Carry a Forex Card

Multi-currency forex cards (HDFC, Thomas Cook) save 3–5% on conversion fees compared to airport exchange counters.

04

Visit Off-Season

Travel in shoulder seasons: Bali in Nov, Europe in Oct, Dubai in May. Hotels drop 40–60% and crowds disappear.

05

Download Offline Maps

Google Maps and Maps.me work offline. Download destination maps before you land — essential in areas with weak signal.

06

Always Carry a Power Bank

A 20,000 mAh power bank is a traveler's best friend. Keep your phone, camera, and earbuds charged on long travel days.
